Common Questions

I can't log in to the app

Verify your credentials with your dispatcher. If you've forgotten your password, use the password reset option on the login screen. If the problem persists, contact us and we'll reset your account.

My location isn't updating

Make sure location permissions are set to "Always" or "While Using App" in your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings > DeMar Logistics > Location. On Android, go to Settings > Apps > DeMar Logistics > Permissions > Location.

I'm not receiving push notifications

Check that notifications are enabled for the app in your device settings. If they are enabled but you're still not receiving alerts, try logging out and back in to refresh your push notification token.

A load isn't showing up in my app

Loads are assigned by your dispatcher. If an expected load isn't visible, confirm with dispatch that it has been assigned to your account. You can also pull down on the loads screen to refresh.

How do I update the app?

App updates are distributed through the Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android). Enable automatic updates in your device settings to always have the latest version.
