DeMar Transportation has both motor carrier (MC) authority and broker authority. They operate their own fleet AND broker loads to partner carriers. They also provide 3PL/warehousing services using a nationwide network of partner warehouses (they don't own the warehouses).

**Why:** The site previously claimed "direct carrier - no middleman markup" and referenced being a Landstar agent. Both were removed (2026-03-29) to avoid misleading customers.

**How to apply:**
- Never say "direct carrier," "no middleman," "no broker markup," or reference Landstar
- Use "asset-based carrier with logistics capabilities," "own fleet and carrier network," "freight partner"
- For warehousing: say "nationwide warehouse network" or "strategic warehouse locations" — never "we own warehouses"
- Target audience: small and mid-market shippers who need freight moved
- Geographic focus: nationwide US
- Differentiator: transparency between partners
